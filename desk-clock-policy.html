Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 04/02/2026

Nothing Desk Clock ("the App"), developed by Apex Creators ("we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our App published on the Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect, store, or require any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location (GPS).

1.1 User-Provided Information

To use the weather feature, you may voluntarily enter a City Name. This information is processed as described in the "Third-Party Services" section below.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide core functionality and advertisements. These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2.1 WeatherAPI.com

To display weather information, the App sends the City Name you enter to WeatherAPI.com.

  • Data Shared: City Name (as entered by you).
  • Purpose: To retrieve real-time weather data for your requested location.
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.weatherapi.com/privacy.aspx

2.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain data to improve ad relevance and app performance.

  • Data Collected:
    • Device information (e.g., model, OS version).
    • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID) - used for ad personalization (if enabled) and frequency capping.
    • Approximate location (inferred from IP address).
    • App performance data (e.g., crash logs, launch time, hang rate).
    • Ad interaction data (e.g., views, clicks).
  • Purpose: To display ads, measure ad performance, and monitor app stability.
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Children’s Privacy

Nothing Desk Clock complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Family Policy Requirements.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
  • The App is configured to serve family-friendly ads.

4. App Permissions

The App requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • INTERNET: Required to fetch weather data from WeatherAPI.com and load advertisements from Google AdMob.
  • AD_ID: Used by Google AdMob for advertising purposes.
  • WAKE_LOCK: Required to keep the screen on while displaying the clock.

5. Data Security

We do not store your personal data on our servers. Any data entered (like City Name) is stored locally on your device or transmitted securely to third-party services (WeatherAPI.com) solely for the purpose of retrieving information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
