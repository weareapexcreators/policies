Welcome to Nothing Notes (the "App"), developed by Apex Creators ("we," "our," or "us").

2. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Nothing Notes stores all notes and app data locally on your device only, including:

We do not collect, upload, or store this data on our servers.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as:

Device information (device model, operating system)

Advertising ID (such as Google Advertising ID)

Approximate location (country or region)

Ad interaction data (such as ad impressions and clicks)


This data is collected and processed according to Google’s privacy policies.
We do not control or have direct access to this data.

Learn more about how Google uses data from apps:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Internet Access & Permissions

The App may use internet access only for displaying advertisements.
All note content and user data remain stored locally on your device.

Locally stored data is used only to display and manage your notes

We do not sell or share your personal data

6. Data Storage & Security

All notes and app data are stored locally on your device
