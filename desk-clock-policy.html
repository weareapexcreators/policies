Aesthetic Desk Clock & Timer is an independent application developed by Apex Creators. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your experience using our app is safe and secure.

1. Definitions

  • App: Aesthetic Desk Clock & Timer
  • Company: Apex Creators ("we", "us", or "our")
  • Device: Any smartphone, tablet, or device capable of running the App
  • Service: The mobile application and its related functions

2. Information Collection

We believe in data minimization. We do not require users to create accounts, provide names, email addresses, or any personal identification to use the core features of the App.

Automatic Data Collection

The App itself does not track you. However, like most mobile applications, certain non-identifying technical data may be collected by standard platform services (Google Play Services) to ensure the App runs correctly:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and performance metrics
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

Weather and Location Data

The App does not collect GPS location data in the background. To display weather information:

  • Users manually enter a city name/location.
  • This city name is stored locally on your device.
  • It is sent to our weather provider solely to retrieve current conditions.

3. Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a mobile advertising platform operated by Google LLC. In compliance with Google's Publisher Policies (updated June 2026), we disclose the following about how AdMob collects and uses your data.

3.1 Data Collected Automatically by the Google Mobile Ads SDK

The Google Mobile Ads SDK (GMA SDK) automatically collects and shares the following data for the purposes of advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention:

  • IP Address: Used to estimate your approximate geographic location for ad relevance.
  • Device Identifiers: Your Android Advertising ID (GAID), used for interest-based ad targeting and analytics.
  • User Interactions: App launch events, ad impressions, and tap interactions, used to measure ad performance and user engagement.
  • Diagnostic Data: Non-personal crash logs and SDK performance metrics used to improve ad delivery stability.

All data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using TLS (Transport Layer Security).

3.2 Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

AdMob may serve personalized (interest-based) ads using your advertising ID. You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

3.3 Advertising ID Controls

You may reset or delete your Advertising ID at any time through your device's ad settings. On Android 13 and above, deleting the advertising ID replaces it with a string of zeros, after which no personalized ads will be served.

3.4 EU / EEA, UK & Switzerland – GDPR Consent

GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App will display a consent dialog before serving personalized ads. This is required under the EU ePrivacy Directive and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as per Google's EU User Consent Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time through the App's settings or your device's ad controls.

3.5 US State Privacy Laws

CCPA / CPRA

For users in US states with applicable privacy laws (including California CPRA, Virginia VCDPA, Colorado CPA, and others), Google AdMob supports Restricted Data Processing (RDP) and honors the Global Privacy Platform (GPP) signal. Where required by law, we limit data processing and honor opt-out requests for the sale or sharing of personal data for targeted advertising.

3.6 Children and Advertising

We do not knowingly serve personalized ads to children under 13. Where required, we apply Google's child-directed treatment tags (TFCD / TFUA) to ensure that the Advertising ID and other identifiers are not transmitted for child-directed ad requests. See also Section 6 (Children's Privacy).
