## 3. Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements served by **Google AdMob**, a mobile advertising platform operated by Google LLC. In compliance with Google's Publisher Policies (updated June 2026), we disclose the following about how AdMob collects and uses your data.

### 3.1 Data Collected Automatically by the Google Mobile Ads SDK

The Google Mobile Ads SDK (GMA SDK) automatically collects and shares the following data for the purposes of advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention:

- **IP Address:** Used to estimate your approximate geographic location for ad relevance.
- **Android Advertising ID (GAID):** A unique, user-resettable device identifier used for interest-based ad targeting and analytics.
- **User Interactions:** App launch events, ad impressions, and tap interactions used to measure ad performance and user engagement.
- **Diagnostic Data:** Non-personal crash logs and SDK performance metrics used to improve ad delivery stability.

All data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is **encrypted in transit using TLS** (Transport Layer Security).

We do not control or have direct access to this data — it is collected and processed by Google according to their privacy policies.

### 3.2 Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

AdMob may serve personalized (interest-based) ads using your Advertising ID. You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your Android device settings:

- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

### 3.3 Advertising ID Controls

You may reset or delete your Advertising ID at any time through your device's ad settings. On Android 13 and above, deleting the Advertising ID replaces it with a string of zeros, after which no personalized ads will be served.

### 3.4 EU / EEA, UK & Switzerland – GDPR Consent

> **GDPR:** If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App will display a consent dialog before serving personalized ads. This is required under the EU ePrivacy Directive and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as per Google's EU User Consent Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time through the App's settings or your device's ad controls.

### 3.5 US State Privacy Laws

> **CCPA / CPRA:** For users in US states with applicable privacy laws (including California CPRA, Virginia VCDPA, Colorado CPA, and others), Google AdMob supports Restricted Data Processing (RDP) and honors the Global Privacy Platform (GPP) signal. Where required by law, we limit data processing and honor opt-out requests for the sale or sharing of personal data for targeted advertising.

### 3.6 Children and Advertising

We do not knowingly serve personalized ads to children under 13. Where required, we apply Google's child-directed treatment tags (TFCD / TFUA) to ensure that the Advertising ID and other identifiers are not transmitted for child-directed ad requests. See also Section 7 (Children's Privacy).

### 3.7 Google's Privacy Policy & AdMob Resources

Data collected via AdMob is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. For full details:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google's Advertising Policies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
- [AdMob Help Center – How AdMob uses data](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
- [How Google uses data from apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
