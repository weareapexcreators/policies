Privacy Policy for LocalAI – Offline AI Chat LLM

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

================================================================================

1. Introduction

Welcome to LocalAI – Offline AI Chat LLM ("the App"), developed and maintained
by Apex Creators ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your
privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your data. Because LocalAI
is designed as an offline inference engine, our privacy practices are
exceptionally straightforward: what happens on your device stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the App. By using
the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

================================================================================

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe that privacy is a fundamental right, especially when interacting with
Artificial Intelligence. To that end, LocalAI is built with a strict
"No Telemetry, No Tracking" architecture:

- No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account, provide an
  email address, or share any personal identity information to use the App.

- No Chat Logs Collection: Your conversation history, prompts, and AI-generated
  responses are stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device.
  We NEVER transmit, collect, or monitor your chat data.

- No Document or Image Uploads: When you use the App's vision or
  document-parsing features, the files and photos you attach are processed
  locally by the AI model entirely on your hardware. We do not upload your
  files to any cloud server.

- No Conversation or File Telemetry: We NEVER collect, monitor, or upload your
  conversation history, prompts, AI responses, images, or documents. All AI
  processing is strictly offline and local to your device.

================================================================================

3. Analytics and Crashlytics

To improve the stability, speed, and performance of the App, we utilize Google
Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics:

- Anonymized Usage Metrics: We log anonymized usage events (such as model
  download starts, download speeds, model load times, and token inference
  speeds) to optimize our on-device memory management guards.

- Anonymized Crash Reports: In the event of a crash, anonymized logs and stack
  traces are transmitted to help us detect and debug system crashes.

- Complete Privacy: No personal identification details, account data, location
  data, or text/image prompts are ever collected or sent to Firebase.

4. Advertising – Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a mobile advertising
platform operated by Google LLC. By integrating AdMob, we are required by
Google's Publisher Policies to clearly disclose how AdMob collects and uses
data when ads are displayed to you.

------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 Data Collected Automatically by the Google Mobile Ads SDK
------------------------------------------------------------

The Google Mobile Ads SDK (GMA SDK) automatically collects and shares the
following data for the purposes of advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention:

- IP Address:
  Your device's IP address, which may be used to estimate your general
  geographic location.

- User Interactions:
  Information about how you interact with the app, including app launches, taps,
  and video views. Performance data such as app launch time, hang rate, or
  energy usage may also be collected to evaluate user behavior and improve
  product features. This data may be used for displaying ads, including sharing
  with other entities that display ads.

- Device Identifiers:
  Your device's advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / IDFA on iOS)
  or other app- or developer-bounded device identifiers, which may be used for
  third-party advertising and analytics.

- Diagnostic Information:
  Non-user-related crash logs and SDK performance data used to diagnose problems
  and improve the SDK. This information may also be used for advertising and
  analytics purposes.

All data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using
TLS (Transport Layer Security).

------------------------------------------------------------
4.2 Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads
------------------------------------------------------------

AdMob may serve personalized (interest-based) ads using your advertising ID and
inferred demographic categories. You may opt out of personalized advertising at
any time through your device settings:

- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > "Delete advertising ID"
           or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
- iOS:     Settings > Privacy > Tracking > disable "Allow Apps to Request to
           Track", and Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > disable
           "Personalized Ads"

------------------------------------------------------------
4.3 Advertising ID Controls
------------------------------------------------------------

Users may reset or delete their Advertising ID at any time through their
device's ad settings. On Android 13 and above, you can delete the advertising
ID entirely, after which it will be replaced with a string of zeros and no
personalized ads will be served.

5. External Network Connections

While LocalAI is primarily an offline application, it does require a temporary
internet connection for:

- The Model Hub: Discovering and downloading open-source Large Language Models
  (LLMs) like LLaMA, DeepSeek, Qwen, and others via Hugging Face
  (huggingface.co).

- Data Exchanged during Downloads: When downloading models, your device
  communicates standard network routing data (such as your IP address) directly
  to Hugging Face. We do not track which models you download.

- Ad Serving: When advertisements are displayed, the Google Mobile Ads SDK
  communicates with Google's ad servers as described in Section 4 above.

Once a model is downloaded to your device, all AI inference requires absolutely
no internet connection.

6. Local Device Permissions

To function properly, LocalAI may request the following device permissions.
All data accessed via these permissions remains strictly on your device, except
where used by Google AdMob as described in Section 4:

- Storage / Files Permission: Required to save downloaded AI models (.gguf
  files), to read documents (PDFs, Word docs, etc.) that you explicitly attach
  to a chat, and to store your chat history locally.

- Camera / Photos Permission: Required only if you choose to take a photo or
  attach an image from your gallery to chat with a Vision-capable AI model.
