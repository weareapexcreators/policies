# Privacy Policy for Nothing Pomodoro Timer

**Last Updated: December 23, 2025**

Apex Creators ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Nothing Pomodoro Timer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
Nothing Pomodoro Timer stores all data **locally on your device**. This includes:
- **User Preferences**: Your display name, avatar, timer durations, and sound settings
- **Task Data**: Tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, priorities, and completion status
- **Session History**: Focus session and break session records for statistics

**We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.**

### No Personal Data Collection
- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- We do not use analytics or tracking services

## How We Use Your Information

All data is used exclusively within the App to:
- Display your personalized settings and tasks
- Track your focus sessions and productivity statistics
- Calculate streaks and performance metrics

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally using SQLite database on your device
- Data is protected by your device's security measures
- We do not have access to any of your data
- Uninstalling the App will delete all stored data

## Third-Party Services

Nothing Pomodoro Timer does **not** integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The App works entirely offline.

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
