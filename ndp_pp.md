# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

**Nothing Daily Planner** ("the App") is developed and maintained by **Apex Creators** ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data Stored Locally
The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Task titles, categories, and completion status
- User preferences (theme, font settings, display name)
- Productivity statistics and insights

**This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

### 1.2 Advertising Data
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data for ad personalization

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 2. How We Use Information

- **Local Data:** Used solely to provide app functionality (task management, insights, personalization). Never shared externally.
- **AdMob Data:** Used by Google to serve relevant advertisements.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

All personal task data is stored locally using encrypted SQLite database on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the App, all local data is permanently deleted.
