Privacy Policy for LocalAI – Offline AI Chat LLM

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to LocalAI – Offline AI Chat LLM ("the App"), developed and maintained by Apex Creators ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your data. Because LocalAI is designed as an offline inference engine, our privacy practices are exceptionally straightforward: what happens on your device stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect
We believe that privacy is a fundamental right, especially when interacting with Artificial Intelligence. To that end, LocalAI is built with a strict "No Telemetry, No Tracking" architecture:

- No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal identity information to use the App.
- No Chat Logs Collection: Your conversation history, prompts, and AI-generated responses are stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. We NEVER transmit, collect, or monitor your chat data.
- No Document or Image Uploads: When you use the App's vision or document-parsing features, the files and photos you attach are processed locally by the AI model entirely on your hardware. We do not upload your files to any cloud server.
- No Conversation or File Telemetry: We NEVER collect, monitor, or upload your conversation history, prompts, AI responses, images, or documents. All AI processing is strictly offline and local to your device.

3. Analytics and Crashlytics
To improve the stability, speed, and performance of the App, we utilize Google Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics:
- Anonymized Usage Metrics: We log anonymized usage events (such as model download starts, download speeds, model load times, and token inference speeds) to optimize our on-device memory management guards.
- Anonymized Crash Reports: In the event of a crash, anonymized logs and stack traces are transmitted to help us detect and debug system crashes.
- Complete Privacy: No personal identification details, account data, location data, or text/image prompts are ever collected or sent to Firebase.

4. External Network Connections
While LocalAI is primarily an offline application, it does require a temporary internet connection for:
- The Model Hub: Discovering and downloading open-source Large Language Models (LLMs) like LLaMA, DeepSeek, Qwen, and others via Hugging Face (huggingface.co).
- Data Exchanged during Downloads: When downloading models, your device communicates standard network routing data (such as your IP address) directly to Hugging Face. We do not track which models you download.

Once a model is downloaded to your device, the AI inference requires absolutely no internet connection.

5. Local Device Permissions
To function properly, LocalAI may request the following device permissions. All data accessed via these permissions remains strictly on your device:

- Storage/Files Permission: Required to save downloaded AI models (.gguf files), to read documents (PDFs, Word docs, etc.) that you explicitly attach to a chat, and to store your chat history locally.
- Camera/Photos Permission: Required only if you choose to take a photo or attach an image from your gallery to chat with a Vision-capable AI model.

6. Third-Party Links
Our App may contain links to third-party websites (such as the Hugging Face website or project repositories). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

7. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect, maintain, or use personal information from children under 13, as we do not collect personal information from anyone. Furthermore, because the App can download and interact with open-source Large Language Models (LLMs) that may generate unpredictable content, we strongly advise that individuals under the age of 18 use the App only under parental supervision. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data somehow, please contact us so we can investigate.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this document. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes; we encourage you to review this policy periodically in the app store or via our website.
